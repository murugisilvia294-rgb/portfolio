Investigated whether G7 countries are meeting climate commitments through emissions analysis, policy comparison and data visualisation using Python.

Key insight: No G7 country is on track for its 2030 target — the UK leads with a 26.1% cut, while Canada's emissions rose 6.5%.

Evaluated county-level readiness for Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum by analysing infrastructure, teacher preparedness and education resources.
